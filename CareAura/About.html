About Us

Welcome to Lumin Aesthetics by CareAura, your number one source for all things beauty and wellness. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of at-home services, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2025 by a team of five passionate individuals, CareAura has come a long way from its beginnings in Panabo City. When we first started out, our passion for helping others drove us to do extensive research and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a thriving business.

We now serve customers all over Panabo City and are thrilled to be a part of the beauty and wellness industry.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our mission

To bring trusted wellness and grooming services to every doorstep with convenience, care, and compassion.

Our values

  • Quality: We partner with certified professionals.
  • Trust: Background checks and transparent pricing.
  • Care: Service designed for comfort and safety.
